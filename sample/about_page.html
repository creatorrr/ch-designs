About us

Overview

HornOK is an online marketplace for pre-owned medium / heavy and light commercial vehicles including trucks, trailers, cranes, buses and tractors.

Mission

Enhance every process involved in heavy moversâ€™ work and business using technology.

Problem

The market for pre-owned heavy commercial vehicles operates through local networks and mouth to mouth channels. The supply is fragmented and localized.

There is a high turnaround time for buying and selling vehicles. It usually takes 3-4 weeks to acquire a vehicle and in some cases, up to 6 weeks to find the right buyer.

The market has nearly zero financial transparency in vehicle valuations. Less influential participants are frequently strong-armed into making sub-optimal deals.

Solution

An online marketplace for pre-owned heavy commercial vehicles to:

  • Increase discoverability of vehicles being sold,
  • Defragment supply and demand by enabling vehicle hubs to interconnect,
  • And increase market transparency
